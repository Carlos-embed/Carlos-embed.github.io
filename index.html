About

Background

Hi, I'm Carlos — a hardware engineer passionate about designing PCBs and building embedded systems that solve real-world problems. I enjoy the full cycle from schematic capture and layout to firmware development and testing.

I'm currently working on personal projects in the PCB and embedded systems space. This portfolio is where I'll document and share those builds as they come to life.

When I'm not at the bench, I enjoy learning new tools, reading datasheets, and looking for creative ways to apply electronics to everyday challenges.
